About the Author

Dr. Paul J. Marc is a biomedical engineer with a broad science and research background spanning biochemistry, mechanical engineering, cancer biology, medicine, and nuclear and radiochemistry. His goal is to bridge the knowledge gap that lies between biomedical engineering Ph.D. researchers and the common man concerning the benefits and hazards of nutritional supplements. Increased health, beauty, and performance are a matter of optimizing the human machine.
